Questions

Frequently asked

Does it work with the block (Gutenberg) checkout?
Yes — Slotora supports both the classic shortcode checkout and the WooCommerce Blocks checkout, registering the date and slot as native Additional Checkout Fields.
Can I make the delivery date optional?
Yes. In WooCommerce → Delivery Slots → General, turn off the “Required Field” option.
What happens when an order is cancelled?
The slot booking is released automatically, freeing that slot's capacity for another customer.
Is it compatible with WooCommerce HPOS?
Fully. Slotora declares High-Performance Order Storage compatibility and stores bookings in its own table.
